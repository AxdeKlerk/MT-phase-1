About This Trial

MOSHTIP® is a live tipping trial supporting artists at ROXOFF.

The artist receives 100% of your tip.

Payments are processed securely via Stripe and no personal data is stored.

DAY 1: Processing fees are covered.

DAY 2: Processing fees are NOT covered. Stripe's payment processor fee of (1.5% + 20p) is added at checkout.

You can choose to use the Autofill link button to save your card details for ease of use during ROXOFF. Your card details are then stored on your device and not on our servers.

If you choose to save your details, verification may be done by email or SMS depending on your device.
